## Core Theoretical Pillars

**1. The Ontology of Flux**
Modern physics suffers from the "Crisis of the Cutoff" (Planck Length limits). DHCM rejects the discretization of space-time, arguing for a strictly continuous hyper-real substrate. It refutes the possibility of static backgrounds (the Void or the Block Universe), proposing instead that existence is the result of inherent instability in a computational flux.

**2. Time as a Calculation Budget**
The framework unifies Special and General Relativity by redefining Time not as a dimension, but as a limited resource. We introduce the **Computational Budget** conservation law. Time dilation is rigorously derived as the exhaustion of processing bandwidth: as an object allocates more bandwidth to spatial negotiation (velocity), it has less remaining for internal processing (time flow).

**3. Mass as Hydrodynamic Viscosity**
Mass is not an intrinsic property of matter but an emergent effect of **Shear-Thickening**. The vacuum acts as a dilatant fluid; high-frequency vorticity (Zitterbewegung) hardens the local vacuum, creating the resistance we measure as inertia. Quantum Mechanics is re-derived as the hydrodynamics of this fractal medium, where particles are stable flow topologies (solitons) rather than point-like objects.

**4. The Dark Matter Resolution: Vacuum Reynolds Number**
Addressing the cosmological crisis, DHCM falsifies the need for particle Dark Matter. We introduce the **Vacuum Reynolds Number** to predict a phase transition in gravitational drag. The theory posits that gravity shifts from Laminar flow (Newtonian) to Turbulent flow (Galactic Halo) at specific scales. This model is supported by recent Gaia DR3 data on wide binaries (Banik et al., 2024), which suggests a breakdown of standard gravity without the presence of dark matter halos.

**5. Syncytial Field Theory (The Physics of Mind)**
SFT bridges the gap between physics and biology. It argues that consciousness is the local biological validation of universal field principles. Rejecting "Quantum Mind" theories that rely on fragile spin states, SFT identifies the brain's **Astroglial Syncytium** as a biological capacitor capable of sustaining macroscopic, classical hydrodynamic standing waves. This continuous ionic field provides the mechanism for binding discrete neuronal spikes into a unified subjective topography (Qualia).
